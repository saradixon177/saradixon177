## Where I have been and where I am going! ✈
**Where I have Been**
* I moved to Colorado for my undergraduate degree and haven't looked back since! While in college, I was an environmental science major with a minor in Asian Studies. While attending school, I had 2 internships that changed the trajectory of my career path. 
* I worked with NOAA on creating tsunami design zone maps in ArcGIS to help identify areas of maximum tsunami flooding in Maui, Hawaii and then used those maps in my senior thesis to create maps that would help indicate the extend and impact of tsunami flooding on drinking water.
* I joined a professor led research project where we used drones, GIS, and climate sensors to analyze the impact climate change is having on grasslands in eastern Colorado 

**Where I am Going**

* I hope to someday use my geospatial and data science skills to better understand and combat climate change. I have always been passionate about understanding our planet and the interactions that exist at the micro and macro level.
